### Needs
Ashley needed a brand from scratch, a way to communicate with her clients and tools to help her manage her business completely online. She uses the website to work with her clients virtually from all over the U.S.

### Responsibilites
Ashley was my client. I handled everything, from gathering requirements, research, wireframing, mockups, design revision and web development.

* Brand Design
* Graphic Design Assets
* WordPress
* Client Portal
* Messaging system
